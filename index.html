Our investment criteria

Sectors

Healthcare, life sciences and pharma

Social Impact

A clearly defined social impact thesis integrated with the business strategy

Path to Profitability

A sustainable business model with a well defined path to profitability

Established Revenue Model

A proven business model with strong revenue traction

Returns Potential

A compelling upside & a fair margin of safety. Ability to chart a tangible growth pathway for value enhancement & exit

Team

Demonstrable commitment of the incumbent management/ promoter toward delivering a positive impact on the vectors Inviga defines

Improved healthcare outcomes

An inherent ability to improve healthcare outcomes on an egalitarian basis

Target Region

Strong & established local market or, in case of growth stage investments, a durable, identifiable, and addressable market.
